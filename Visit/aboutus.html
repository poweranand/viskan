About Us  
 
The visitkanchi.org team comprises of members with various professional backgrounds who have felt the need to highlight and promote Kanchipuram as a tourist destination. The team provides continued support to make this portal better with special focus on increasing and improving the content. New spots, festivals and legendary person details are added every week to this site and we encourage tourists to visit this site frequently.

While we have identified several areas of improvement, we did not want to delay the launch of this useful tourist portal. Any help in improving this website is most welcome and you may write to us at info@visitkanchi.org
