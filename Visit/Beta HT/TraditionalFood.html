Kancheepuram idly is specially prepared as an offering to Lord Varadaraja Perumal at the Kancheepuram temple. The spiced up idly batter is steamed in baskets ( kudalai ), hence it is also known as KUDALAI IDLY. Steaming the batter in banana leaf cups ( donnai ) adds an extraordinary flavour to Kancheepuram idlies. These idlies can also be cooked directly on the idly plates, just like the normal idlies. But lining the idly plates with greased banana leaf strips will impart the special aroma to the Kancheepuram idlies. These idlies are served hot with chutney, molaga podi or onion sambar.
